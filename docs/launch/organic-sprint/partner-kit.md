# WillBuddy free Texas estate-planning tools: partner kit

Updated: 2026-07-13

## What WillBuddy is

WillBuddy helps Texas families prepare for estate-planning conversations. Its free educational tools organize common questions about readiness, wills and trusts, intestacy, planning costs, and incapacity documents. WillBuddy is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice.

## Why a partner might share these resources

- Every tool gives an educational result without requiring payment, an account, or an email address.
- The tools are Texas-focused and link to primary legal sources where appropriate.
- Each tool states its limits and encourages attorney review for situation-specific advice.
- The library can help a partner answer an adjacent audience question without turning the partner into a legal adviser.

## Claims that require revision before use

Do not publish any of these statements:

- “Get a legally valid Texas will.”
- “Avoid probate.”
- “Save thousands.”
- “Know exactly who inherits.”
- “Attorney approved,” unless the named reviewer, date, scope, and permission are documented.
- “Anonymous survey,” if the deployed analytics configuration or exported event data contains identifiers beyond the published privacy description.
