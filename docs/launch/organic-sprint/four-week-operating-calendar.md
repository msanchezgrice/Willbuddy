## End-of-sprint decision rules

- Continue a partner segment when it produces at least two positive replies or one live relevant placement from a manually reviewed first cohort.
- Rewrite the offer when opens/delivery are healthy but replies show the audience benefit is unclear.
- Stop a segment after two carefully personalized cohorts produce no positive signal.
- Expand a video topic when it generates tool clicks or relevant search impressions, not merely views.
- Continue paid search only when the end-to-end conversion event is trustworthy and cost is within the separately approved threshold.
- Do not launch broad awareness ads until there is a proven conversion path and a specific incremental-reach question to answer.
