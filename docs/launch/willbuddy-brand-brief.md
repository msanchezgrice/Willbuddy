# WillBuddy Brand Brief

Date: 2026-06-27

## Positioning

WillBuddy is the voice-first, affordable preparatory layer for Texas estate planning. It helps busy families finish the difficult discovery work conversationally, then produces organized draft documents for attorney review.

## Audience

- Texas parents and couples who know they need a will but keep avoiding long forms.
- Families who want to decide guardianship, healthcare wishes, powers of attorney, and asset direction before meeting an attorney.
- Budget-conscious households that still want a lawyer-reviewed final estate plan.

## Core Promise

Talk through the hard decisions naturally, capture the nuance, and leave with attorney-ready draft materials instead of an unfinished worksheet.

## Differentiators

- Voice-first conversation instead of form fatigue.
- Built around Texas estate-planning preparation.
- Empathetic guidance without legalese.
- Draft document output is positioned for licensed attorney review, not as legal advice.

## Messaging Guardrails

- Do say: draft documents, attorney review, Texas-focused, conversation, preparation, clarity.
- Do not say: legal advice, lawyer replacement, guaranteed validity, nationwide compliance, court-ready without attorney review.
