# WillBuddy Ad Campaign — Static Ads

**Campaign theme:** *Talk it out before you lawyer up.*
**Audience:** Texas couples with young kids (parents 28–45)
**Core insight:** A lawyer is expensive and intimidating. Before you spend $1,000–$2,000, there are big questions you and your partner need to actually talk through. For $49, WillBuddy lets you take as long as you want, thinking it through with a trained AI coach — then walk into the lawyer's office prepared.

---

## Brand Voice (applied)

WillBuddy is the **knowledgeable friend on the couch**, not the attorney behind the desk.

**Voice attributes**

- **Warm, not clinical** — We talk about kids, worry, and "someday," not "testamentary instruments." *Sounds like:* "Who raises your kids if you're not there?" *Not:* "Designate a testamentary guardian."
- **Calm and reassuring** — We reduce dread, never manufacture panic. We acknowledge this is hard and make it feel doable. *Sounds like:* "You don't have to have the answers yet. That's the point." *Not:* "Don't die without a will!"
- **Plain-spoken and honest** — Real numbers, no jargon, no fake urgency. *Sounds like:* "A lawyer starts at $1,000. WillBuddy is $49." *Not:* "Save THOUSANDS instantly!!!"
- **Encouraging, never guilt-tripping** — We nudge busy parents kindly. The pitch is "this matters and it's finally easy," not "you're a bad parent if you wait."

**Guardrails (compliance)**

- Never call it legal advice or imply we replace an attorney. WillBuddy prepares you *for* a lawyer.
- Texas only — never imply national coverage.
- Use "draft documents," "prepare," "get ready" — not "legally binding," "official will," or "execute."
- Price is **$49**, free to start. Attorney comparison: **$1,000–$2,000**.
- No emojis in headline/primary text on Meta/Google. Light, optional emoji acceptable on organic Instagram/X only.

**Messaging pillars**

1. **Talk it out** — big decisions get made in conversation, not forms.
2. **Come back to it** — no pressure, pause and resume, revisit anytime.
3. **Think it through together** — for couples, on your own time.
4. **Walk in ready** — hand your lawyer an organized kit; pay for review, not discovery.
5. **Don't put it off** — you have little kids; this is the future that matters most.
